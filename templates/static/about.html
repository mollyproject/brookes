About Brookes Mobile

Mobile Brookes is being deeveloped through a collaborative JISC-funded project involving Oxford University and Oxford Brookes University. The aim is to implement Oxford University's online portal for mobile devices, Mobile Oxford (the product of a previous JISC- funded project), in a different institution and explore the adaptations required to make it work effectively in its new context.
